## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I book for a non-Renaissance fair event?**  
Absolutely. We specialize in Renaissance fairs, but The Merchant Table, Quarterdeck, and Captain's Reception work anywhere atmosphere and theming enhance the experience. Think themed parties, weddings, upscale events, or dedicated cigar lounges.

**What if some guests don't want cigars or pipes?**  
The experience is designed to welcome all—smokers and non-smokers alike. Many guests enjoy the merchant storytelling and atmosphere without purchasing. We never pressure.

**Can you customize the selection for my event?**  
Yes. We'll discuss your guest profile and occasion, then curate cigars, pipes, and tobacco blends that fit. Whether mild for newcomers or bold for experienced smokers, we match to the room.

**Do I need to provide seating or setup?**  
For The Merchant Table (fair booth), no—it's self-contained. For Quarterdeck or Captain's Reception, seating and a dedicated venue area enhance the experience significantly. Let's discuss your space.

**How early can you arrive for setup?**  
Setup varies by tier (30 min to 2+ hours). Contact us with your event timeline and we'll confirm.
