Whether you are at a lounge pop-up or a Renaissance fair, etiquette keeps the experience better for everyone.

## The Basics

- Ask before lighting in tight spaces
- Keep ash controlled and avoid flicking into walkways
- Don't block entrances while smoking
- Respect event and venue rules first

## Conversation Etiquette

- New smokers may ask beginner questions; keep answers welcoming
- Don't gatekeep cigar or pipe choices
- Share recommendations, not pressure

## Good Merchant Rule

The best smoking spaces are social and respectful. If your behavior makes others uncomfortable, adjust.
