## The Right Start

Toast the foot first. Rotate slowly. Draw gently. Avoid rushing the first minute.

## Cadence Rule

One puff every 45 to 60 seconds is a great baseline for most cigars.

## Fixing Burn Issues

- Uneven burn: touch up the low side
- Heat spike: pause for 1 to 2 minutes
- Harshness: slow your pace and reduce draw pressure
