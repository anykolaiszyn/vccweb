# About The Black Leaf Bounty

The Black Leaf Bounty is the Renaissance merchant house within Vice City Cigars: a premium tobacconist presented with a bit of stagecraft and a steady hand.

## The Origin Story
What began as a love of premium tobacco eventually split into two worlds. One became modern tropical luxury. The other became The Black Leaf Bounty, a traveling merchant identity shaped for Renaissance fairs, pirate revels, and guests who enjoy a little atmosphere with real product knowledge.

## What Sets The Bounty Apart
- Recommendations shaped by tobacco knowledge instead of generic sales scripts
- A merchant-captain voice that welcomes newcomers without talking down to them
- Pipe education and blend guidance alongside premium cigar selections
- A booth presence designed to feel like a proper trading post instead of a generic retail tent

## Our Promise
No hard sell. No hollow pirate caricature. Just carefully chosen goods, practical guidance, and a memorable merchant experience built on actual tobacconist standards.

## The House Character
Expect charcoal, parchment, rope-tan, and weathered wood rather than neon or nightclub gloss. Expect language that sounds like an experienced captain speaking to travelers. Expect every page and event appearance to treat tobacco like a craft worth learning, not merely a prop.

> Welcome aboard the Black Leaf Bounty. The ship carries cigars of character, pipe leaf worth savoring, and counsel for those still learning the trade winds.
