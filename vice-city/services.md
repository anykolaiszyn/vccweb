## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much does a custom event booking cost?**  
Pricing depends on guest count, event duration, travel distance, and cigar selection. Typical bookings range $500–$2,500+. Contact us with your event details for a no-obligation quote.

**What's the minimum guest count?**  
We recommend a minimum of 15 guests. Smaller, intimate events are possible—reach out to discuss options.

**Can you accommodate dietary restrictions or smoke-free zones?**  
Absolutely. We'll work with your venue to set up in a designated smoking area that works for all guests. Some venues have designated cigar lounges or outdoor areas we can utilize.

**Do I need to provide anything?**  
No—we bring everything: humidor, cigars, cutters, lighters, and guidance. Your job is to enjoy and host your guests.

**What if a guest doesn't want a cigar?**  
No pressure. Vice City is about the experience, not the sale. Guests can enjoy the atmosphere, the company, and guidance without smoking.

**Are there eco-friendly or tobacco-free alternatives?**  
Our focus is curated tobaccos, but we're happy to discuss your event needs. Contact us to explore options.

**How far in advance should I book?**  
We recommend 2–4 weeks minimum, especially for popular dates. Peak seasons (holidays, summer) fill faster. Sooner bookings are possible—ask about availability.

**What about weather?**  
Most setups work indoors and outdoors. Let us know your venue, and we'll confirm feasibility.
