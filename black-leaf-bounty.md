## About the Bounty

Black Leaf Bounty is the pirate-themed sub-brand of Vice City Cigars, born from a love of adventure, history, and the romance of the high seas. At Renaissance Fairs across South Florida, we transform tobacco retail into theatrical experience—where every cigar selection feels like a treasure discovered on a distant shore.

Step into our booth expecting gold-trimmed display cases, period-appropriate atmosphere, and a selection of obscure, premium, and fascinating tobacco products you won't find in your everyday shop. This is where history meets craft. Where the adventure of tobacco comes alive.

**Why Black Leaf Bounty?** Because the finest tobacco has always been passed between adventurers, traders, and rebels. We're honoring that legacy.
