## Key Differences

Pipes require prep and cadence management. Cigars are simpler to start and easier for social events.

## Time Commitment

- Pipe: variable, depends on bowl and cadence
- Cigar: usually predictable by vitola and draw pace

## Which Should You Try First

If you want the easiest social start, begin with cigars. If you enjoy ritual and slower setup, explore pipes.
