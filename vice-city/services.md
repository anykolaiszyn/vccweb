## Frequently Asked Questions

**How much does it cost?**  
Pricing is quote-based and depends on guest count, event duration, and cigar lineup. Most custom experiences begin around mid-tier event pricing and scale with scope.

**What do I need to provide?**  
Usually just the space and event details. We bring the cigar hospitality setup.

**Can you help if my guests are beginners?**  
Yes. Beginner guidance is part of the experience.
