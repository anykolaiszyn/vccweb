A simple humidor setup beats a complicated one you never maintain.

## Quick Setup

- Keep target RH around 65-69%
- Keep temperature stable around 65-72 F
- Avoid direct sun and car trunks
- Use distilled water for maintenance

## Common Mistakes

- Over-humidifying to 75%+ (causes swelling and poor burn)
- Opening the humidor constantly
- Mixing very different cigar profiles in small storage

## Simple Rule

Consistency matters more than perfection. A steady environment preserves draw, burn, and flavor.
