## The Pairing Story

Every venue has one. Here's how it usually goes:

**Craft Brewery:** Stouts and Maduros. IPAs and Connecticuts. The craft beer and cigar pairing is one of the most underutilized in the premium beverage world. Your regulars are ready for it.

**Distillery:** Bourbon and cigars. Dark rum and cigars. The oldest pairing in luxury hospitality. Your guests already know this combination &mdash; they just need somewhere to enjoy it.

**Winery:** Port and Maduro. Cabernet and Nicaraguan. Champagne and Connecticut. Guided pairing experiences your guests will photograph and remember. SOBEWFF wine brands &mdash; this is your activation.

**Other Venues:** Every category has a pairing story. Tell us about your space and your audience. We'll find it.
