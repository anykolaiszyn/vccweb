Use this quick list before heading out.

## Bring

- Cutter or pipe tool
- Lighter (and backup)
- Cleaner(s) for pipes
- Small pouch or case
- Water to stay hydrated

## If You Smoke Pipes

- Pre-pack one backup bowl for convenience
- Bring pipe cleaners for in-session maintenance
- Keep tobacco in a sealed pouch

## If You Smoke Cigars

- Pack by session length (short, medium, long)
- Keep one "safe" cigar you already enjoy
- Avoid bringing only full-bodied options in heat

Prepared smokers have better sessions and fewer hassles.
