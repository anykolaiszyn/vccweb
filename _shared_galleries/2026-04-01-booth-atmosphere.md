The Black Leaf Bounty booth is part of the experience. Weathered wood, rope, parchment signage—it feels discovered, not manufactured. Every guest who walks up gets a sense that this isn't a generic costume booth; it's a real merchant space where the goods matter and the guidance is honest.

The booth layout changes slightly between fairs (space and layout vary), but the core atmosphere stays the same: a trading post aesthetic, organized by tobacco family, with the Captain at the center ready to help. 

First-timers often say the booth feels like stepping into a real merchant's hold. That's exactly the intention.
