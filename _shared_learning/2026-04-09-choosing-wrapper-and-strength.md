## Wrapper Quick Guide

- Connecticut: mild, creamy, approachable
- Habano: medium body with spice and depth
- Maduro: richer, sweeter, often fuller profile

## Strength vs Flavor

Strength describes nicotine impact. Flavor describes taste complexity. A cigar can be strong with simple flavor, or mild with layered flavor.

## Recommendation

If you are unsure, start mild-to-medium and adjust after your first two sessions.
